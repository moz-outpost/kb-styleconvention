  1. Launch Jive app on your phone. Figure 1: Picture of computer
  2. Click the hamburger icon on the upper-left corner. Figure 2: Picture of computer again
  3. Click Contacts, search by name or extension number on the search bar at the top.
  4. Click the contact you would like to add to favorites.
  5. On the contact information windows, hit Add to Favorites at the bottom.
  6. To remove the person from favorites, click Remove From Favorites
